Title: E-Commerce: Evolution, Trends, And Future Prospects

Authors: Dr. Ranjit Satish Kadam, Prof. Nikhil Khandve

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-03-30

Description:

<em><span>E-commerce has transformed the global economy by changing how businesses operate and how consumers shop. The rapid development of digital technology, combined with increasing internet penetration, has enabled businesses to expand their reach beyond traditional markets. This paper explores the evolution of e- commerce, various business models, key technological advancements, challenges, and future prospects. The role of artificial intelligence (AI), block chain, and big data in shaping the future of online commerce is discussed, along with the emerging trends such as automation, voice commerce, and sustainable business practices.</span></em>
