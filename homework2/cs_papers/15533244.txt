Title: Emerging Trends in Commerce & Management

Authors: Mr. Nikhil Khandve

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-03-30

Description:
<div>

<em><span>The landscape of commerce and management is experiencing significant transformation due to advancements in technology, globalization, and evolving consumer expectations. Digitalization, artificial intelligence (AI), blockchain, and sustainability initiatives are reshaping commercial activities, while management practices are shifting toward data-driven strategies, remote work adaptability, and corporate social responsibility (CSR). This paper explores the latest developments in commerce and management, analyzing their influence on business operations, the challenges they introduce, and potential future implications.</span></em>
